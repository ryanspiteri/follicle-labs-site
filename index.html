How It Works

The MPC Inhibition Pathway

Understanding the mechanism is the first step to understanding why this class of compounds generates serious scientific interest.

  • 1

    Follicle Stem Cells Enter Quiescence

    High MPC activity keeps hair follicle stem cells (HFSCs) in a dormant state. Dormant follicles do not produce hair. This is the problem minoxidil cannot solve.

  • 2

    JXL-069 Inhibits the MPC

    Applied topically, JXL-069 inhibits the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier — the metabolic gatekeeper that keeps HFSCs quiescent. This shifts cellular energy metabolism toward glycolysis.

  • 3

    Stem Cells Activate

    The metabolic shift triggers HFSC activation. Activated stem cells re-enter the hair growth cycle. Dormant follicles begin producing hair again.

  • 4

    Observable Results

    In PP405 trials, measurable hair density increases were observed at week 8 with only 4 weeks of active treatment. Results persisted beyond the active treatment window.
