01 / The Science

How It Works

Step 01
MPC Inhibition
JXL-069 blocks the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier in hair follicle stem cells, shifting their metabolism from oxidative phosphorylation to glycolysis.
Step 02
Stem Cell Activation
This metabolic shift wakes dormant follicles. Ki67 marker upregulation confirms rapid cell proliferation within 24-48 hours of first application.
Step 03
Follicle Transition
Dormant follicles in telogen phase are pushed into anagen (active growth). This is the mechanism no other topical treatment targets.
Step 04
Terminal Hair Growth
Phase 2a data confirmed growth of terminal (thick, visible) hair in previously bald areas — not just vellus hairs like minoxidil produces.
