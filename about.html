Research-Grade Supply for Serious Researchers

Hairstemlab was built around a single frustration: the research chemical market for hair follicle science in Australia is full of low-quality supply, unverified purity, and vague sourcing claims. We built the supply chain we wanted to buy from.

Why We Exist

In 2024, the PP405 Phase 2a trial results came out of UCLA and Pelage Pharmaceuticals. The data — 31% of participants showing more than 20% hair density increase after just four weeks of active treatment — generated significant interest in MPC inhibition as a mechanistic target.

As demand for research-grade MPC inhibitors grew, supply in Australia fragmented. Products appeared claiming to be JXL-069 or "PP405 analog" with no purity verification, inconsistent concentration, and no formulation detail. For researchers, that is worthless. Worse, it is misleading.

Hairstemlab was established to supply a single product correctly: JXL-069 at 0.05% concentration, third-party HPLC verified, formulated specifically for topical research application, dispatched from Australian facilities.
