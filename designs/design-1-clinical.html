Mechanism

How MPC-1 works

MPC-1 targets the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier pathway, shifting follicle stem cells from a quiescent state into an active growth phase.

1

MPC Inhibition

Selective inhibition of the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier shifts cellular metabolism in follicle stem cells.

2

Stem Cell Activation

Metabolic shift pushes quiescent hair follicle stem cells toward proliferation and differentiation.

3

Follicle Cycling

Activated stem cells re-enter the anagen (growth) phase, supporting increased hair density over time.
