The Mechanism

How MPC-1 works

MPC-1 is a selective inhibitor of the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier, a mechanism linked to the reactivation of quiescent hair follicle stem cells in emerging clinical research.

I

MPC Inhibition

Targets the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier to shift cellular metabolic state in follicle stem cells.

II

Stem Cell Waking

Metabolic reprogramming of quiescent stem cells toward active proliferation.

III

Growth Phase

Follicle stem cells re-enter anagen, supporting measurable increases in hair density.
